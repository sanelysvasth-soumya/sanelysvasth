Vegetable Ragi Dosa/Chilla

A nutritious pancake enriched with ragi, fresh vegetables and chickpea flour.

Prep Time
20 mins

Cook Time
30 mins

Calories
120 kcal per chilla

Ingredients

  • Ragi flour - 1/3 cup
  • Carrot - 1 small size
  • Bengal gram flour - 3 tsp
  • Onion - 1 small size
  • Capsicum - 1 small size
  • Cabbage - 4 small leaves
  • Oil - 1 tbsp
  • Coriander leaves - Few
  • Ginger paste - Â½ tsp
  • Water - 150 ml (approx.)

Instructions

  1. Chop vegetables and blanch for five minutes.
  2. Mix ragi, Bengal gram flour, salt, and green chili to make a batter by adding water.
  3. Heat oil, add green chilies and chopped onion, stir-fry till transparent. Add blanched vegetables and stir-fry for 5 minutes with the lid on.
  4. Add coriander leaves to the fried vegetables.
  5. Divide the fried vegetables into six equal portions.
  6. Heat a tawa, grease it with oil, put a ladleful of batter, and spread it.
  7. Cook for a few seconds and turn the side.
  8. Spread a portion of fried vegetables over it.
  9. Cook for a few seconds, roll it, and serve with chutney.
