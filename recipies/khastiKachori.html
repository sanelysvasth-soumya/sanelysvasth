Khasta Kachori

A crispy and flavorful kachori stuffed with spiced dal and spinach.

Prep Time
2 hrs 30 mins

Cook Time
20 mins

Calories
200-220 kcal

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup whole wheat flour
  • 2/5 cup oil (absorbed)
  • 1/4 cup black gram dal
  • 1/2 cup spinach
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp mango powder
  • 1/4 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/4 tsp carom seeds
  • 1/4 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Soak dal for 2 hours, drain water completely, and grind coarsely.
  2. Steam spinach and grind it.
  3. Add spinach paste to flour and make smooth dough.
  4. Heat 1 tbsp oil, add all spices, and stir for a minute.
  5. Add ground dal, sugar, salt, and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Divide fillings and dough into 8 equal portions.
  7. Roll out each dough portion into a small roti, place one portion of dal filling in the center, bring the edges together, and press firmly.
  8. Roll the stuffed dough into a puri.
  9. Heat oil and deep-fry till golden on both sides.
  10. Serve hot with chutney or yogurt.
