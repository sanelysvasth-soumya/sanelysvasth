Quinoa Upma

A healthy and flavorful upma made with quinoa and vegetables.

Prep Time
10 mins

Cook Time
15 mins

Calories
180-220 kcal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa (rinsed)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 carrot (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup peas
  • 1 green chili (slit)
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Lemon juice (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a pan, heat oil and add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions, green chili, and saut√© until golden.
  3. Add chopped carrots and peas and cook for a couple of minutes.
  4. Add rinsed quinoa, turmeric powder, and salt, and mix well.
  5. Add 2 cups of water, cover, and cook on low heat for about 10-12 minutes until the quinoa is tender and the water is absorbed.
  6. Garnish with coriander leaves and squeeze some lemon juice before serving.
